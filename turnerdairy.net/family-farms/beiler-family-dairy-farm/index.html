Hopeway Dairy

At Hopeway Dairy in Westmoreland County, multiple generations of the Frye family work together every day to keep their family farm running. Partners with Turner's since 1975, the Fryes manage approximately 1,000 acres of farmland while caring for and milking their herd of roughly 250. The farm sits atop a ridge with a view of farms as far as the eye can see - fields filled with corn, silos and bright red and white barns dot the landscape.
